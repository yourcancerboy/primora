Questions

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before travelling with Primora Escape Co.

Primora Escape Co. is based in Batam, Kepulauan Riau, Indonesia. We serve travellers from Singapore and Malaysia seeking curated, seamless journeys across Indonesia.
Ferries depart daily from HarbourFront Terminal, Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal, or Woodlands Ferry Terminal. The ride takes around 45–55 minutes. We can arrange your ferry tickets as part of your package — just let us know.
No. Singapore and Malaysian passport holders enjoy visa-free entry to Indonesia (including Batam) for up to 30 days. Just make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from your travel date.
The official currency is Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). Some tourist spots accept SGD, but you'll get much better rates changing to IDR. We recommend exchanging cash at a reputable money changer in Batam upon arrival — rates are generally very favourable.
Each package is stated clearly in your quote: accommodation, land transfers, guided experiences, and optional ferry tickets. What is included and excluded is always communicated upfront.
We accept PayNow (Singapore), DuitNow (Malaysia), and bank transfer. A deposit of 30–50% secures your booking, with the balance settled before travel. A confirmation and receipt are issued upon payment.
Yes. We specialise in group and corporate trips. Whether you have 10 or 200+ pax, we handle ferry arrangements, group hotel rates, team activities, and full logistics. WhatsApp us for a custom corporate quote.
Batam is our home base and signature destination. We also arrange Bali (available now), with Bromo, Komodo, Labuan Bajo, and Raja Ampat coming soon. Send us a message and we will confirm availability.
We understand plans can change. Please contact us as early as possible if you need to reschedule or cancel. Cancellation terms vary depending on supplier policies (hotels, ferries), but we will always work with you to find the best solution.
We aim to reply within 1 hour during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9am–9pm). The most direct way to reach us is via WhatsApp. For complex itineraries, we'll send a full quote within 24 hours.
