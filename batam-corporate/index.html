Why Batam

The Practical Corporate
Retreat Destination

For Singapore companies, Batam removes most of the complexity that makes corporate retreats difficult to organise and expensive to execute.

01

No Flights Needed

Ferries from HarbourFront depart throughout the day. A team of 40 can be in Batam, checked into their hotel, and having lunch within 3 hours of leaving the office. No flight bookings, no baggage allowance complications, no airport terminal logistics.

02

Significant Cost Savings

A comparable retreat in Bali or Thailand involves flights, international hotel rates, and peak-season pricing. In Batam, group hotel blocks are well-priced, the ferry is SGD 70–80 return, and group dinners at waterfront seafood restaurants cost a fraction of equivalent catering in Singapore.

03

Real Activity Variety

Golf tournaments, beach water challenges, go-kart racing, cooking classes, spa afternoons, structured team-building programmes with facilitators — Batam has enough variety that mixed teams with different preferences can all find something worthwhile.

Activities

Team Building Activities in Batam

We curate the right activity mix for your team's size, preferences, and objectives — then arrange and coordinate everything.

⛳

Golf Tournament

Scramble or stableford format across one of Batam's top courses. Works for mixed skill levels — non-golfers can caddie or join for the 19th hole. Always well-received.

🏄

Beach Water Challenges

Relay races, kayak challenges, beach volleyball, and inflatable obstacle courses. High energy, easy for mixed groups, and universally enjoyed regardless of fitness level.

🏎

Go-Kart Racing

Batam has a proper go-kart circuit. A timed competition format generates genuine competitive energy in a fun, low-pressure environment. Good for afternoon entertainment.

🍳

Indonesian Cooking Class

Learn to make rendang, nasi goreng, and satay as a group. A distinctive, hands-on activity that works well as a team bonding experience — and ends with a meal together.

🎯

Facilitated Team Programme

For companies with specific team development goals: structured programmes covering communication, collaboration, and leadership, delivered by experienced local facilitators.

🦞

Group Seafood Dinner

A long waterfront seafood dinner is often the highlight of the whole retreat. Live-catch crabs, prawns, lobster and fish at a fraction of Singapore prices — with a view and the space to actually talk.
