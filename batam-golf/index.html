Top Courses

Batam's Best Golf Courses

Batam has over 12 international-standard courses. These are the four we most frequently recommend for visitors from Singapore.

Tering Bay Golf & Country Club

Top Rated

Consistently rated among the best courses in Indonesia. 18 holes to international standard, excellent course conditions year-round, and sea views from multiple fairways. The clubhouse is comfortable with good facilities. Best for golfers who want a premium round at a fair price.

Batam Hills Golf Resort

36 Holes

A 36-hole facility — two full courses — making it ideal for full-day play or groups wanting variety. The courses wind through natural terrain with sea glimpses from higher elevations. Excellent for groups who want to play different layouts across a weekend stay.

Palm Spring Golf & Beach Resort

Most Scenic

Batam's most scenic course — set along the beachfront with ocean views from most holes. A slightly more relaxed atmosphere than Tering Bay, but well-maintained and spectacularly located. The beach resort facilities are a bonus after your round.

Kabil Golf Course

Best Value

A solid, well-priced option for groups or golfers looking for a straightforward, uncrowded round. Less scenically dramatic than the top courses but reliable and at the most competitive green fee in Batam. Good for warm-up rounds or budget-conscious groups.
